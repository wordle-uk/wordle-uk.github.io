How to Play

In Wordle UK, the objective is straightforward: guess the hidden five-letter word within six attempts. Each guess will be highlighted with colors indicating how close your guess is to the correct word. Green indicates correct letters in the right position, while yellow means correct letters in the wrong position. Use these clues strategically to deduce the word before you run out of guesses!

Strategy Tips

To maximize your chances of guessing the word correctly, consider starting with vowels and common consonants in your first few guesses.
